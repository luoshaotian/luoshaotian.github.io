what is the aim of researching thermoelectrics ?

we want to get thermoelectrics materials which are cheap,notoxic,abundant.However, for inorganic thermoelctrics materials,it don’t meet the conditions. So,nowadays,we are devoting to find the way to develop organic thermoelectrics materials(OTEs),its merit can be described by ZT:

$$ZT=\frac{S^2\sigma}{k}T$$

——S: seebeck coefficient

——$\sigma$: electrical conductivity

——k: thermal conductivity

——T: absolute temperature

we can elevate its magnitude via $\sigma$ and S,because its k already very small,since they form soilds through van der waals(read carefully) interaction with poor latticle thermal conductivity.

How to improve ZT magnitude ?

For $\sigma$: $$\sigma=qn\mu$$
– q: carrier charge

– n: charge carrier concentration

– $\mu$: electron mobility.

organic semiconductors usually have low charge carrier concentration thereby low $\sigma$,but we can chage it via chemical doping.

While in the process of doping, doping efficiency and doping stability is of improtance.

doping efficiency will be disturbed by mismatch of energy level,poor miscibilityand so forth. And,seebeck coefficient show reverse relationship with $\sigma$ which is still under debate.

For $\sigma$

Energy level

we wish there are appropriate engery level offset between OSCs and dopants,for p-doping, the dopant’s LUMO energy level should belower than the HOMO energy level of the OSCs.while for n-type doping, the dopant’s HOMO energy level should be higher than the LUMO energy level of the OSC. But it also exits special case:

  • ionized dopant: coulomb interaction make up the engery mismatch.
  • charge transfer …
  • chemical reaction.

For n-doping, it needs high reducing ability, good air stability, adequate miscibility. lowering LUMO energy level of it is a decent way.

supplementary:doping methods$\rightarrow$field-effect transistor

Miscibility

Miscibility can strongly influence the doping efficiency. we can use below methods to obtain high miscibiliy:

  • optimizing polymer structure
  • create new n-dopant

counterion

Doping will produce polaron,bipolaron, and onized dopants which all will influence mobility.
At low doping level, separated couterions form the steep coulomb traps thus lead to low mobility.

  • by mixing different materials to lower the effect of coulomb interaction.
  • If with high doping level, counterion interaction is negligible and polymer crystallinity is of importance.

Doping stability

Doping stability include air stability and operation stability.
For air stability:

  • lower LUMO engery level of polymer
  • use certain kind of encapsulation
    For operation stability, there are new researches for it.
  • ground-state charge transfer phenomenon: low ionization conjugated polymer mix with polymer with high electron-affinity.
  • use polymer as dopant rather than small molecules(low mass,low stability).
  • change dopants’ structure via blending,twisting and so on.

For seebeck coefficient

Above this article, we already describe the definition of S. Accroding to Mott’s transport theory,it also connects with the slope of DOS near the Fermi level.$$ S= \frac{dlng(E)}{dE}$$
So, if we try to improve S, we can start with DOS.
Normally, the higher slope of DOS, the bigger S.Its theory still needs further development.

structure disorder

structure disorder can be described via paracrystallinity g,you can get a good number of $\sigma$ without sacrificing S. All of methods to change paracrystallinity can be used to change $\sigma$.

Dopant clustering

Dopants tend to aggregate to form cluster. For getting high S with low influence to $\sigma$, we should control the size of dopant clusters.

polaron and bipolaron

Above this article, we already mentioned that polaron/bipolaron will cause low stability.However, if we rightly tune the solid-state molecular packing, it will form the special semi-metallic structure. So, the effects of polaron/bipolaron are still under controversy.
